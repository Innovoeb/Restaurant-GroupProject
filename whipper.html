About Us

Start them young and let your kids have a night on the town! Bring them down to The WhipperSnapper where we serve the best kiddie cocktails, ice cream floats, and culinary dishes that will please the senses as well as satisfy the appetite. Our service is given under the guise of a neighborhood dive-bar complete with a full seated sized bar and a 30 table dining room. In our house, whippersnappers now have a place where they can socialize with their families and peers while taking in a beverage establishment's atomosphere and enjoying well crafted foods and non-alcoholic drinks. Come see what the fuss is about down at The WhipperSnapper.

We started with an idea that kids should have a night on the town too. With this, we set out to slightly alter an older food service concept. A place where youngsters can enjoy food & beverage and simultaneiously be social, alot like the old jukebox ice cream parlors of past. We Took this concept, added a sohphisticated menu complete with adult sized and whippersnapper sized dishes, and came up with "The WhipperSnapper".

We are located at 1112 S Casino Center Blvd, Las Vegas, NV 89104 and we host groups and parties as well as accept reservations. Contact us at (702) 560-5326 for more information. You can also schedule a reservation by filling out and submiting our form in the reservations tab and we will contact you shortly after to confirm. Don't hesitate to bring your whippersnappers in today for some clean fun and we look forward to seeing you soon.
