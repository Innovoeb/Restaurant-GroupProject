About Us

We started with an idea that kids should have independent fun too. In our house, kids can socialize with their peers while eating delicious foods. While your kids are eating and socializing, parents can feel at ease knowing that their children are well supervised while you run errands. We provide excellent meal service given under the guise of a dive-bar restaurant atmosphere. We offer kid-sized finger foods and kiddy cocktails, with exceptional service.

Hours: Weekdays: Lunch (12:00pm-3:00pm) Weekdays: Dinner (5:00pm - 8:00pm) Weekends: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner served all day (9:00am - 9:00pm)
